The bloody ginger Special

If you want gingers eating ribs with bloody sauce dripping from their hands then you're in the right place. Many people have this once and never want anything else again.

Ingredients:

  • Foot crushed tomato sauce
  • fermented onion water
  • Ribs from any mammal
  • The presence of a ginger

Steps:

  1. Lower the ribs into a pot of boiling onion water
  2. Begin to round up gingers for the tomato crushing
  3. Once items are aquired, commence the tomato stomping ritual
  4. The moment they are no longer a solid you collect the juices and put in onion water
  5. Let the dish ferment for 2-4 weeks
  6. Enjoy the ribs!
