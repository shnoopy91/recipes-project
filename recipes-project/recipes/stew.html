Baby Shned Stew

If you have a taste for sweets as well as pasta, you're at the right place.

With this homemade dish created by the one and only Shned Kakamodo it's hard to expect nothing less than greatness

It consists of minimal ingredients yet tastes like you're eating out the rainbow

Ingredients:

  • Mr. noodles
  • Kit Kat bar
  • Chipotle Mango spice

Steps:

  1. Begin boiling the water
  2. Once water boils delicately lower the noodles in
  3. Have Shned lick all the chocolate off the Kit Kat
  4. Once noodles are cooked, get shned to regurgitate the chocolate onto the noodles
  5. Enjoy the Baby Shned Stew!
